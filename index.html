This project sets up a website, with the goal of helping you deal with stress.

Every person will experience some form of stress in their life. It can be moderated, and people can work to avoid it as much as possible, but a person will still have to face a stressful situation or idea regardless. The important thing to remember is that not all stress needs to, or will, be prolonged. One of the best things that anyone can do is to learn how to cope with stress.
