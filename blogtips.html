Advice & Tips

At this point you have reached the site either knowing, or learning more about, stress and how it can affect your body. There are many ways to manage stress. Not all of the ways to manage stress will work for every person, so it is important to be patient with yourself while you try to figure out what works best for you.

MayoClinic lists some ways to deal with stress:

  • Cardio
  • Pilates
  • Swimming
  • Dancing
  • Yoga
  • Meditation
  • Deep Breathing
  • Talk with Friends
  • Meet with or call Family
  • Play with Pets
  • Reading
  • Painting
  • Playing card or Board Games
  • Listening to Music

Note: If you think you or someone you know may have anxiety or depression, it is recommended to speak with a medical professional.
