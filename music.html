Music

Music is considered to be a universal langauge. Not only that, but it can evoke many different emotions from listeners. For for those of you who are like me and struggle with anxiety (or those of you that struggle with the general stresses of life) music can be a way to relax and center yourself. Podcasts

Podcasts can be created by anyone, and some do discuss how to handle stress and can give advice on everyday life. I listed some below, but if these don't appeal to you, there are always others that you can look up!

Working with Anxieties

The Science of Happiness is a podcast that is intended to use the science behind mindfulness, gratitude, etc. in order to talk about strategies that allow people to live happier lives.

Connecting with Others

The Daily is a podcast created by the New York Times that talks about a variety of topics. This podcast is spesifically about how people are using technology to stay connected with friends and family during quarantine.

Caring for Yourself

In this podcast, the speakers of Ten Percent Happier talk about being forgiving towards yourself during significant life events such as the COVID-19 outbreak.
